Medical Visa to India

Patients who are seeking medical treatment in India can available e-visa medical facility for travel to India. This visa can be applied online on Government of India website , minimum 4 days in advance of the date of arrival with a window of 120 days.The payment for the visa can be done online along with the application and Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) will be sent to the individualâ€™s e-mail id. Please check here to see if you are eligible for e-visa and also the fee associated with the application.

Eligibility for e-visa:

  • International Travellers whose sole objective of visiting India is recreation, sightseeing, casual visit to meet friends or relatives, short duration medical treatment or casual business visit.
  • Passport should have at least six months validity from the date of arrival in India. The passport should have at least two blank pages for stamping by the Immigration Officer.
  • International Travellers should have the return ticket or onward journey ticket, with sufficient money to spend during his/her stay in India.
  • International Travellers having Pakistani Passport or Pakistani origin may please apply for regular Visa at Indian Mission.
  • Not available to:
    • Diplomatic/Official Passport Holders or Laissez-passer travel document holders.
    • Not available to individuals endorsed on Parent's/Spouse's Passport i.e. each individual should have a separate passport.
    • Not available to International Travel Document Holders.

Documents required for application:

  1. Scanned pdf version of the first page of applicant's passport. The file size should between 10KB to 300KB.
  2. Copy of Letter from the Hospital concerned in India on its letterhead.Medinovita will work with hospital to get the letter for the patient to apply for the medical visa.
  3. A digital photograph in JPEG meeting following requirement is also needed:
    • File Size should be 10KB to 1MB
    • The height and width of the photo should be equal.
    • The photo should have a full face, front view and eyes open.
    • Center head within the frame. Head from top of the hair to the bottom of the chin of the individual should be prominent.
    • The background should be plain light colored or white.
    • There should be no shadows on the face or on the background.
    • The photo should not have any borders.

Other details:

Applicant should carry a copy of the e-visa during his or her travel and e-Visa is valid for entry through 24 designated airports & 3 seaports in India.Traveller should ensure that they are booking the flights to following airports:
Cochin, Trivandrum, Calicut, Coimbatore, Ahmedabad, Amritsar, Bagdogra, Bengaluru, Chennai, Chandigarh, Delhi, Gaya, Goa, Guwahati, Hyderabad, Jaipur, Kolkata, Lucknow, Mangalore, Mumbai, Nagpur, Pune, Tiruchirapalli, and Varanasi. They can take the exit from any of the authorized Immigration Check Posts (ICPs) in India.

Biometric details of the applicant will be mandatorily captured at Immigration on arrival to India and the visa is valid for 60days from the date of arrival in India.E-visa facility can be availed two times in a calendar year and is non-extendable, non-convertible and not valid for visiting Protected/Restricted and Cantonment Areas.Nationals of Yellow Fever affected countries must carry Yellow Fever Vaccination Card at the time of arrival to India else they may be quarantined for 6 days upon arrival.
